Everything You Need to
Manage Keywords Like a Pro

Keyword Capture

One-click capture of keywords directly from Google Ads and Keyword Planner. Supports Broad, Phrase, and Exact match types.

🤖

AI Keyword Suggestions

Generate relevant keyword ideas powered by DeepSeek AI. Get hundreds of smart suggestions based on your seed keywords.

Ad Copy Generator

Generate 20 headlines, 8 descriptions, and 8 CTAs with value-based messaging, emotional triggers, and FOMO — all with proper title case.

🚫

Search Term Analysis

Automatically analyze search terms to identify negative keywords, high-performing terms to add, and pause candidates.

Performance Analysis

Track keyword performance metrics, filter by impressions, clicks, conversions, and identify optimization opportunities.

🔍

Planner Analyzer

Import and analyze Keyword Planner data. Organize by volume, competition, and suggested bid for smarter campaign building.
